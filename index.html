TEDxSMVDU | The Next Chapter

TEDx SMVDU aims to create a TED like environment in order to bring new ideas and innovations to action and nurture the flame of curiosity within people.TEDx SMVDU is an independently organized TEDx event after prior permission and license from TED.

TED

TED is a nonprofit devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. It started out (in 1984) as a conference bringing together people from three worlds: Technology, Entertainment, Design. Since then its scope has become ever broader. Along with two annual conferences - the TED Conference in Long Beach and Palm Springs each spring, and the TED Global conference in Edinburgh UK each summer - TED includes the award-winning TED Talks video site, the Open Translation Project and TED Conversations, the inspiring TED Fellows and TEDx programs, and the annual TED Prize.

TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized. (Subject to certain rules and regulations.)


Speakers


DR. BHUVNESH KUMAR

Dr. Bhuvnesh Kumar is the director of Defence Institute of High Altitude research (DIHAR), Leh. Throughout a career spanning more than two decades, he has worked on developing cutting-edge technologies to augment the supply of fresh food to troops deployed at extreme altitudes throughout the year. Since taking charge of DIHAR in 2015, he has been directing similar efforts to strengthen the Siachen Glacier base and Tawang situated in the eastern Himalayas. He has previously served at various strategic locations like Pithoragarh, Kinnaur & Lahul Spiti bordering Indo-Tibet and Nepal


BABAR AFZAL

Being a revered hacker and working as a International Business consultant is a dream for many people. That is exactly what Babar Afzal left behind to become a shepherd in the Himalayas to save the Pashmina Goats. A Pashmina Activist, Afzal has highlighted issues linked to climate change to climate change and injustice in the pashmina eco-system across Himalayas . Widely credited as the most credible voice on Pashmina in the world, he was recently profile by TIME magazine in it’s “Genius Issue”, a feat achieved by only 10 people in the past decade.


Dr. SWARKAR SHARMA

Dr. Swarkar Sharma, is an active researcher in the field of genetics, who primarily focuses on understanding the genetics and biology of rare and common human disorders. He holds a PhD in human genetics, and joined the Department of Biotechnology, SMVDU after a completing a Postdoctoral fellowship in Human Molecular Genetics from USA. Recently, his research team has discovered mutations in a gene causing a very rare skeletal disorder. He has also undertaken research challenges in the state of Jammu and Kashmir, targeting scarcely understood genetic disorders. He has contributed in various outstanding scientific research projects as is evident by his publication records.


NAVDEEP SINGH GILL

Mr. Navdeep Singh Gill is an Entrepreneur, Founder and CEO at XenonStack, a firm which builds data-driven enterprises solutions and RayFarm, a marketplace where farmers can sell directly to consumers. With an experience of over 13 years and a passion for design and data, he firmly believes that technology can be used to enrich our lives. He loves to help and guide young professionals for working with technologies like BigData ,IoT and DevOps.


KALPESH GAWALI

Kalpesh Gawali is the CEO of Nutrino Energy Pvt. Ltd, a company which aims to address energy security challenges by coming up with renewable energy solutions for the urban sector. He has previously worked in the energy sector for more than a couple of years at places like Reliance Industries Ltd. Having come up with a unique initiative of ‘Renewable Energy Skill Development”, Mr. Gawali has been recognised as the second youngest certified climate leader in India after being trained by Al Gore, a credible voice in the domain of climate change. He continues to represent India on various international platforms pertaining to climate change.


WALTER PETER

Mr. Walter Peter is a Theater Educationist who is currently the Artistic Director of the TIESociety, New Delhi and TIE-INTERNAIONAL, Ahmedabad . He is a proponent of the concept of “Theatre in Education”, wherein education is imparted through means of staged drama. He is also the Ex-TIE Co. member of National School of Drama, New Delhi and recently had a fellowship from CRY on Change through TIE Process. Peter has directed more than 50 plays, developing original scripts for them. Poetry has also been an area of special interest for him.


YUVRAJ BHARDWAJ

Yuvraj Bharadwaj is a Researcher, Roboticist, Technologist, inventor and a full stack developer. But the most remarkable thing about him is his age. At 16, he has worked on 22 research projects along with his twin brother Yashraj, 7 of which are being filed for patents. Research projects credited to his name include smart water purifiers, drones, portable Angiography machines, portable all in one medical assistance machine and a Bajra Purifier for Removal/ Extraction of heavy Metals From River Water Using Bajra.


SANJEEV NEWAR

Sanjeev is a prolific writer, speaker and entrepreneur who writes on contemporary topics, social issues, spiritualism and Indian scriptures. Founder of Agniveer - a popular website and social media platform dedicated to these topics, he is also a data scientist whose risk management solutions have garnered acclaim throughout the world. These include several inclusive breakthroughs like developing the longest endurance light-weight drone from India, a bio-stimulant to increase productivity of leafy vegetables without impacting the soil, biodegradable batteries for the common man, a signaling system to prevent railway track accidents amongst many others.


AAKASH RANISON

Akash Ranison, according to himself, is a traveller by heart and Entrepreneur by choice. He generally travels by means of cycling or hitchhiking, having covered 18000 kms cycling and another 48000 kms hitchhiking visiting more than 80 cities across the length and breadth of the country. At age of 15, he founded the “The Golden Bird Foundation”, which has reached and affected over 8,000 people in Indore District through their Projects, Campaigns & Events. Holder of certificates from EC council and Red Hat, he loves to dabble into technology that reaches out to people and places, sans boundaries.


Dr.Suyogkumar Bhavare

Dr. Suyog Bhavare is Chief Operating Officer (COO) of Jagriti Yatra, a social programme wherein participants undertake a 15-day long, national train journey across India, to understand and figure out ways to build the country through enterprise. A doctor by profession and a social entrepreneur by heart, he's relentlessly driven towards contributing in the social development sector. He also spearheads two NGO’s which work for improving health & education. His primary venture, chain of clinic named as Aarogyam Chikitsalaya (AYURVA), focuses on providing dedicated activities in creating awareness about child birth healthcare system.


RITESH GARG

Ritesh is a social entrepreneur whose work revolves around the Himalayas and the youth of country. An alumnus of the Banaras Hindu University, he founded the I for Nation Foundation and Yuva Prerna Yatra. He has travelled throughout the country in quest of issues on the grass root level. Having been a part of various disaster relief efforts, he worked on livelihood development in the Kedarnath Valley in the aftermath of 2013 Uttarakhand Floods. He also did his bit to help the victims of the 2015 Nepal earthquakes by building houses for over 2000 people with an unique design and concept.


SACHIN JAIN

Mr. Sachin Kumar Jain is the Director of Vikas Samvad Human Development Resource and Advocacy Organisation. He has written over 1500 articles concerning issues of social importance like Poverty, Hunger, Democracy, Governance and Identity. An author of 41 booklets/primers on issues like Food, Hunger, Gender, Food Security, Entitlements, MGNREGA, Health and Nutrition, displacement, migration, climate etc, he has published 13 fact-finding reports on social exclusion, displacement, migration, malnutrition etc.. An active campaigner for Right to Food, he is also an Advisor to the Supreme Court Commissioners for the same.

We Are Here

Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University is situated 40 Km North of Jammu in the scenic surroundings of Trikuta foothills, near the holy town of Katra in District -Reasi. The Campus is situated close to the beautiful tourist resort of Jhajjar - Kotli. One can reach SMVDU campus by local or private transport from Jammu.

Option 1: Catch Taxi Directly from Ralway Station / Bus Stand / Airport directly to University Campus (Distance approximately 45 Kms.)

Option 2: Board bus from Jammu Bus Stand/Railyway Station plying to Udhampur/Katra but alight at Domel (Approx. 39 Kms.). Board Mini Bus fom Domel to University (Approx. 6 Kms.) In the map given below SMVDU CAMPUS can be seen as the small black box.
